# MoneyFactor Vertical Rules

## Active engine
BestCardsForMe is the active validation engine.

## Core Strategy
Google/SEO is the near-term revenue engine.

AI citation + MoneyFactor brand trust is the long-term enterprise value moat.

BCFM remains the active validation engine.

No new BCFM production edits unless triggered by:
- GSC data
- affiliate requirements
- production issues

MoneyFactor should build toward being the trusted “honest financial decision math” brand across a small number of high-stakes personal finance verticals.

The moat is not raw AI content volume. The moat is:
- honest financial decision math
- structured methodology
- transparent assumptions
- calculators/tools
- trust pages
- author credibility
- affiliate/disclosure clarity
- cross-vertical authority
- brand mentions
- AI citation visibility

## Strategy doctrine
MoneyFactor is adopting an AI-scaled multi-vertical preparation strategy, but not full chaotic parallel deployment.

Principle: parallel preparation, sequential deployment.

AI can compress production timelines, but editorial quality, methodology consistency, GSC validation, and affiliate fit remain gating factors.

Google organic traffic is the near-term revenue engine. AI citation optimization plus MoneyFactor brand trust is the long-term enterprise value path.

MoneyFactor should build toward being the trusted honest financial decision math brand across a small number of high-stakes verticals.

MoneyFactor should prove:
1. BCFM = first validation engine
2. LeaseFactor = second repeatable engine
3. third vertical = proof that the model generalizes

Do not launch 4+ verticals at once.

## AI/GEO doctrine
MoneyFactor’s moat is honest financial decision math, structured methodology, calculators/tools, transparent assumptions, trust pages, and cross-vertical authority — not raw AI content volume.

AI citation moat backlog:
- methodology pages
- calculator/tool IP
- structured comparison tables
- trust signals
- author credibility
- affiliate/disclosure clarity
- brand mentions
- future citation tracking

## Launch trigger for next vertical
Do not launch LeaseFactor or any new vertical until BCFM shows one or more of:
- consistent organic clicks
- affiliate approval
- first revenue
- sustained 500+ impressions/week with multiple pages showing

## Vertical qualification criteria
A MoneyFactor vertical must:
1. represent a high-intent financial decision
2. involve confusing math or tradeoffs
3. support structured comparison content
4. be monetizable via affiliate, lead gen, or data-driven monetization
5. fit the MoneyFactor “real math” framework
